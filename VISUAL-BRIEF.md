# VISUAL BRIEF — NEXARA (Showcase Template)

## Brand Identity
- **Name**: NEXARA
- **Archetype**: Futuristic Tech Pioneer
- **Personality**: Bold, visionary, commanding, electric

## Brand Shape
- **Primary**: Hexagonal fragments — tech, structure, precision
- **Secondary**: Dot grids — data, connections, neural networks

## Typography
- **Font**: Discovery FS Variable (100-900)
- **Display**: Weight 900, size clamp(4rem, 12vw, 14rem), line-height 0.88

- **Labels**: Weight 400, tracking 0.3em, uppercase, opacity 0.5
- **Weight Drama**: 100 (ghost/bg numbers) vs 900 (headlines)

- **Background**: #06060a (deep dark, cold blue-black)
- **Foreground**: #e8e4dc (warm cream)
